General Questions

What is Leaf Guard?

Leaf Guard is an AI-powered plant disease detection platform that helps farmers, gardeners, and plant enthusiasts identify and treat plant diseases quickly and accurately. Our system uses advanced image recognition technology to analyze photos of plants and provide instant diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

How accurate is Leaf Guard?

Leaf Guard has an average accuracy rate of 95% for common plant diseases. Our AI model has been trained on millions of images and is continuously improving through machine learning. However, accuracy may vary depending on image quality, lighting conditions, and the specific disease. For critical cases, we always recommend consulting with a professional agronomist or plant pathologist.

Which plants and diseases does Leaf Guard support?

Leaf Guard currently supports over 60 plant species and can identify more than 120 common plant diseases. Our database includes most common crops (tomatoes, potatoes, corn, wheat, rice, etc.), fruit trees, ornamental plants, and common garden vegetables. We're constantly expanding our database to include more plants and diseases. You can check the complete list in our Plant Database section.

Is Leaf Guard available in my country?

Yes! Leaf Guard is available worldwide. Our platform is accessible from any country with internet access. We currently support 12 languages, and we're working on adding more to make our service accessible to as many people as possible.

Disease Detection

How do I take good photos for accurate disease detection?

For the best results, follow these guidelines:

  • Take photos in natural daylight (avoid direct sunlight or flash)
  • Make sure the affected area is in focus and clearly visible
  • Include both healthy and diseased parts of the plant for comparison
  • Take multiple photos from different angles if possible
  • Avoid shadows or glare on the plant
  • Keep the background simple to help the AI focus on the plant

Can Leaf Guard detect nutrient deficiencies?

Yes, Leaf Guard can identify common nutrient deficiencies such as nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, magnesium, and calcium deficiencies. However, some nutrient deficiencies can look similar to certain diseases, so our system will provide the most likely causes and recommend soil testing for confirmation in ambiguous cases.

What information does Leaf Guard provide after detecting a disease?

After detecting a disease, Leaf Guard provides:

  • The name and description of the identified disease
  • Confidence level of the diagnosis
  • Detailed information about the disease's cause (fungal, bacterial, viral, etc.)
  • Recommended treatment options (organic and conventional)
  • Prevention tips to avoid future occurrences
  • Similar diseases that might be confused with the diagnosis
  • Links to additional resources and research

Can I use Leaf Guard offline?

Yes, Leaf Guard offers limited offline functionality. You can download a lightweight version of our AI model for the most common plant diseases. This allows basic detection without an internet connection. However, for the most accurate results and access to our complete database, an internet connection is recommended. The offline mode is available for premium users only.

Technical Questions

Which devices and browsers are supported?

Leaf Guard works on most modern devices and browsers:

  • Mobile: iOS 12+ and Android 8.0+
  • Browsers: Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge (latest 2 versions)
  • Desktop: Windows 10+, macOS 10.13+, Linux (major distributions)

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Safari on a device with a good camera.

How does Leaf Guard handle my data and privacy?

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Plant images are stored securely and used only for disease detection and improving our AI model
  • Location data is used only for providing localized recommendations and is never shared with third parties
  • We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or other companies
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Is there an API available for developers?

  • Plant database access
  • Treatment recommendation retrieval
  • Comprehensive documentation and code examples
  • Developer support

For more information and API documentation, visit our Developer Portal.
